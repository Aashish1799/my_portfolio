1. IoT-Driven Smart Irrigation with Moisture-Dependent Pump Control for Sustainable Agriculture

In the face of climate change, depleting water resources, and the increasing demand for agricultural production, the efficient management of irrigation has become an urgent concern. Conventional irrigation methods often lead to excessive water consumption, resulting in environmental degradation and economic losses. Moreover, the unpredictability of weather patterns and shifting rainfall patterns compounds the issue, creating additional complexity as farmers find it challenging to adjust their irrigation strategies to cope with these variable conditions. This pressing problem necessitates innovative solutions to bridge the gap between water scarcity and the need for increased agricultural productivity. This paper brings forth the idea of an IoT-based smart irrigation system that integrates threshold values and real-time rainfall forecasts to address the fundamental challenges of water management in agriculture. By harnessing IoT technology, this system efficiently transmits sensor data to a cloud-based platform, providing an intuitive user interface for manual pump control. This system's incorporation of threshold values and user customizable automation has the potential to significantly reduce water wastage, enhance crop yields, and ultimately pave the way for a more sustainable and efficient approach to agriculture.
